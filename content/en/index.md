::biography{title="Our Story"}
The Rocking Coasters are more than just a band; we're a time machine. Based on a wordplay where coasters are ships rocking on waves, and the coasters beneath your favourite drink while a record spins, we bring the authentic 50s and 60s rock 'n' roll experience to life. From high-energy dancing tunes to smooth ballads, we guarantee a night you won't forget.
